Custom Operating System

  • We started the year developing in three directions at once: AP reporting tools, AP core device software, and a custom operating system with our manufacturing partner.
  • That third path has now succeeded, unlocking meaningful cost savings while still delivering a quality product for our field partners.
  • Our close relationship with the manufacturing partner also gives us an inside track for future updates and for scaling production quickly if needed.

SUCCESSFUL

Reporting Across Networks

  • Our Pccess Points now successfully send reporting data over SIM connections across every major network type: 3G, 4G, 5G, and LTE.
  • This means the device reports reliably no matter what network generation is available at a given field location.
  • This gives us confidence that reporting will keep working as we deploy to more places with different network conditions.
